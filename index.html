Trung Tran

I’m an AI enthusiast, currently working as a Data Scientist at Pixta Vietnam. I am also the Chief Technology Officer at ClientScan and a co-founder of TakeNote.ai, both based in Oxford, UK. I’m passionate about turning AI into practical solutions. My main interests are in foundation models, computer vision, vision-language models, voice processing, and chatbots. I hold a Master’s degree from Politecnico di Torino, where I was mentored by Prof. Fabio Dovis. Prior to that, I earned my CS engineering degree from HANOI UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY in Vietnam.

Contact: trungtranthanh1990 at gmail dot com
